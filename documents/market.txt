We have been locals here eating here for just about 5 years.  The food, when it is done right, is actually fantastic.  It is a bummer that the prices have gone up significantly over the years, especially for a primarily self serve/take out location.  Over the years and most especially the last two years eating here,  we have had multiple order mistakes such as missing chowder for chowder fries and missing tartar sauce when they charge extra for extra tartar sauce.   Pretty disappointing for a place that also requests gratuity when consistency is not confident from our experiences.  We are more than happy to give  more opportunities, but it is getting harder to order the crab roll and the lobster roll, which i personally love and recommend to all our friends and family.  The last time i had a crab roll, there were multiple obvious shell pieces that were very concerning from a safety standpoint.  Today i had the lobster roll and there were dangerously large shell pieces including one shell horn that could have done some serious damage to me, and most especially a child.  To me this is unacceptable regardless of the cost ($36).  I have attached pictures as proof.  Again, i understand prices have went up due to a national cost issue - I’m just disappointed that quality control has gone down despite the higher cost premiums.  I hope this review from a long time local return customer can serve as an opportunity to get Market back to the quality control and consistency I feel their food deserves.
