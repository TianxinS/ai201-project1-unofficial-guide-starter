I don't normally take time to write reviews, especially in Washington where they cover everything in terriyaki glaze. This is one of the hidden gems of Mexican food in the Puget Sound. They have the pickled carrots, onion, radish, delicious salsas to include reftrita. I've only tried it recently on a late night whim, but the last two weeks it's what we crave in my house when we want Mexican food and we've gone in person. Alibertos and California Burrito cannot hold a candle to this place and I wish the people I knew directed me here earlier. The quality of meat and tortilla alone is superior. Also, adding chipotles to the house salsa, I see you!

Unfortunately, this was a bit dissappointing for us! When one pays more than $60 for Combo #66 (2) Gorditas, 1 Taco - Carne Molida (Ground Beef), a Side of Rice and Beans, and Ench Chipotle (3)iladas one has a few expectations. One would expect the food to arrive hot or even warm but ice cold in foil containers not at all. So now not only do we have to put everything in the microwave but we have to transfer all of it to a microwave safe container first. Then we realized there were no limes, cheese, or any sour cream included with the order either. Also, there were very clear requests, also printed on my receipt, where it clearly states no lettuce, onion, tomato, or cabbage and yet there was still lettuce, onion, and cabbage. I wanted so badly to find something redeeming so what I can say is the salsa verde is delicious! It is not too spicy where one is unable to taste all of the little flavorful nuances in the making of the sauce! So, if you give these guys a shot then do yourself a favor and try the green sauce!! Otherwise, once I removed the unwanted ingredients and heated them, the Combo #66 (2) Gorditas had good flavor too. I wish I could say the same about the taco or enchiladas. I have eaten here previously and it was absolutely a different person in the kitchen tonight. Anyway, I unsure if I will try again sometime in the future but probably not any time soon. Enjoy your night and your meal everyone!!
