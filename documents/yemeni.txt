Suhel Banerjee
4 months ago
After coming across a few glowing reviews of this newly opened restaurant up north, we decided to visit it with our foodie group. Called a day in advance and got a reservation for a large group for a Friday dinner. Turns out the place was packed with a line out the door.

The menu includes traditional yemeni dishes e.g. mandi rice, galada, agda with options for chicken, lamb, and fish. While the wait for the food was a bit long (they're still in early stages after a recent opening) each and every item was simply outstanding. The meats (lamb, chicken, and fish) were tender and falling off the bones, the dishes were the perfect level of spice and flavor. And all of it was served with a smile.

Sharing a few photos below. This restaurant has jumped to the top 5 restaurants in Seattle area list for me and will revisit soon!
